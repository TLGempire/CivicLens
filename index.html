Why is local legislation hard?
📄
No standard API
Federal and state governments have structured data feeds. Local governments mostly publish PDFs to websites with no consistent format.
🔄
Thousands of jurisdictions
There are over 90,000 local government entities in the US, each with its own process for publishing legislative information.
🛠️
Our approach
We're building scrapers and partnering with local government platforms to bring this data in reliably. Starting with top 50 metro areas.
